Frequently Asked Questions

What does this cost our property?

Nothing. We provide, install, stock, and maintain the market at no cost to you. We generate revenue from product sales and your property is the host.

How often do you restock?

Restocking frequency is based on your foot traffic. Most locations receive two to three visits per week. High-volume sites can receive daily service.

How much space is required?

A minimal setup fits in as little as 60 square feet. We have outfitted break rooms, hallway alcoves, and full lobby corridors and adapt to whatever space is available.

Can tenants request specific products?

Yes. We have a request process and rotate in popular items. Your property manager can also provide us with a preferred product list at the time of setup.

What happens if the iPad has an issue?

Each kiosk has a QR code sticker that gives shoppers immediate access to the full web version of the store with secure payment processing. This ensures purchases can continue without interruption while we resolve any hardware issue.

Ready to add a market?

Tell us about your property and we will reach out within one business day to schedule a free site visit.
